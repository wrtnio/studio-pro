**Step 1:** Search for ‘WRTN Studio Pro’ on Google and access the login page, or visit the following link. ([https://studio-pro.wrtn.ai](https://studio-pro.wrtn.ai/login))

![](/studio-pro/images/docs/getting-started/register-and-login/step-1-1.png)

**Step 2:** Click the login button.

![](/studio-pro/images/docs/getting-started/register-and-login/step-2-1.png)

**Step 3:** Select the service you want to use to sign up and log in to the product. If this is your first time using the service, you will be registered as a new user. If you have already signed up, you will be logged in and redirected to the home screen.

![](/studio-pro/images/docs/getting-started/register-and-login/step-3-1.png)

**Step 4:** Log in and select the ID you want to use for the service.

![](/studio-pro/images/docs/getting-started/register-and-login/step-4-1.png)

**Step 5:** Check the terms of the service box and click the Complete Registration button.

![](/studio-pro/images/docs/getting-started/register-and-login/step-5-1.png)

**Step 6:** Click the Start button (black button).

![](/studio-pro/images/docs/getting-started/register-and-login/step-6-1.png)

**Step 7:** You will be redirected to the Studio Pro product home screen.

![](/studio-pro/images/docs/getting-started/register-and-login/step-7-1.png)

## Language settings

**Step 1:** Hover over the left navigation bar and access the settings page.

![](/studio-pro/images/docs/getting-started/language-settings/step-1-1.png)

![](/studio-pro/images/docs/getting-started/language-settings/step-1-2.png)

**Step 2:** Click on the second item ‘Change Language / 언어 변경’ and select your preferred language from the dropdown menu. (Currently, Korean and English are supported.)

![](/studio-pro/images/docs/getting-started/language-settings/step-2-1.png)
