Interface IHubSaleReview

Reviews for listing snapshots.

IHubSaleReview is a subtype entity of IHubSaleInquiry, and is used when a customer purchases a listing (a snapshot at that time) registered by a seller as a product and wants to leave a review and evaluation for it.

Note that IHubSaleReview and IHubOrderGood are algebraically N:1, but that does not mean that a customer can continue to write reviews for the same product indefinitely.
