Interface IStudioEnterprise

Corporate entity.

IStudioEnterprise is an entity that represents a company, and literally means a subject participating in the studio system as a company unit. And in the case of a company, unlike IHubMember members, it must have a account.

In addition, a company has IStudioEnterpriseEmployee employees and teams composed of them as sub-entities. And among these teams, its IStudioEnterpriseTeamCompanion members can have the same IStudioRepositoryAccess access rights of the IStudioRepository repository belonging to the company as a group.
