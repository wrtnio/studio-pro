Interface IHubCouponCriteriaOfFunnel

Limiting the inflow path of discount coupons.

IHubCouponCriteriaOfFunnel is a subtype entity of IHubCouponCriteria, and is used when you want to issue or exclude IHubCoupon discount coupons only to customers who come from a specific path.

And limiting the inflow path is possible in three ways as follows. First is IHubCustomer.referrer, and in addition, by parsing IHubCustomer.href, which records the customer's access address, restrictions can be applied to specific URLs or variable units.
