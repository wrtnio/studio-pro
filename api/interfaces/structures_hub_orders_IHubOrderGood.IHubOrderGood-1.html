Interface IHubOrderGood

Individual product information that constitutes an order.

IHubOrderGood is an entity that represents each product ordered by a customer, and the record is created when product raw materials in the shopping cart are upgraded to products due to a customer's order request.

And IHubOrderGood is a concept that corresponds to the snapshot unit of the product, whether it is IHubCartCommodity or not.

In addition, IHubOrderGood is the most basic unit for the process after the order, that is, after-sales service (A/S). For example, the unit where a customer issues an issue for an ordered product or requests a refund is IHubOrderGood.

opened_at

opened_at: null | string & Format<"date-time">

The time of commencement of the order contract.

Unlike the time of confirmation of the order, the effective date can be postponed later than that. And the effective date of the contract can be continuously edited until it arrives.

Also, the monthly fixed fee is calculated based on the commencement date.

Please note that after the customer purchases the seller's API, the process of reviewing and developing it is necessary, so the effective date of the original contract cannot help but be postponed later than the order confirmation.
