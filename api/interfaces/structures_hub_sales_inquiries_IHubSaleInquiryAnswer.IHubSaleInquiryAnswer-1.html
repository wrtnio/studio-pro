Interface IHubSaleInquiryAnswer

Answer to the inquiry in the listing snapshot.

IHubSaleInquiryAnswer is an entity that embodies the official answer written by the seller to the inquiry written by the customer. Of course, in addition to writing an official answer like this, the seller can also communicate with the questioner and multiple customers through comments in the attributed inquiry.

Comments cannot be written on the answer. You should encourage comments to be written on the inquiry.
